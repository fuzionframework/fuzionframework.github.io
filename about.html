History

Originally created by a web developer and an Engineering Student in BML Munjal University, Fuzion is a merger of Bootstrap and Materialize which has been created out of necessity to get the best of both frameworks.

Fuzion was created in BML Munjal University in mid-2016 by MZed. I don't need to tell you why the name Fuzion. It is still under development phase

Originally released on To Be Declared .

Team

Brand guidelines

Have a need for Fuzion's brand resources? Great! We have only a few guidelines we follow, and in turn ask you to follow as well. These guidelines were inspired by MailChimp's Brand Assets.

Mark and logo

Use either the Fuzion mark (a capital F) or the standard logo (just Fuzion). It should always appear in Roboto Bold. Do not use the Twitter bird or Google logo in association with Fuzion.

Name

The project and framework should always be referred to as Fuzion. No Bootstrap or Material before it and no abbreviations except for one, a capital F.

Colors

Our docs and branding use a handful of primary colors to differentiate what is Fuzion from what is in Fuzion. In other words, if it's green, it's representative of Fuzion.
